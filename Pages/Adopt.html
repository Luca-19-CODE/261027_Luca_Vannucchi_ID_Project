Your Jovrney with a little legend begins here.

optional starter packages made just for your creature: tiny beds, treats, toys, and more. You can even get multiple packages if your

legend will have indoor and outdoor spaces. After you complete your application, we'll arrange a magical meeting so you can see

which creature is the perfect fit. We've also included a beginners friendly rating with our creatures so that you can gauge if you are

ready to make them a part of your family.
